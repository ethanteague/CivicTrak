Building Permit Application Checklist

City Building Department

Use this checklist to ensure your application is complete

Required Documents

☐ Completed building permit application form

☐ Property survey or site plan showing setbacks

☐ Building plans and drawings (2 sets)

☐ Structural engineering plans (if required)

☐ Contractor license and insurance certificate

☐ HOA approval (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Submit completed application with all required documents
  2. Pay applicable fees (calculated based on project cost)
  3. Wait for plan review (3-5 business days)
  4. Receive permit and inspection schedule
  5. Schedule required inspections during construction

Fee Schedule

Project Type Base Fee Additional Costs
Single Family Residence $200 + $2 per $1,000 project cost
Addition/Renovation $100 + $1.50 per $1,000 project cost
Accessory Structure $75 + $1 per $1,000 project cost
